Mobile Pet
Ultrasound

A pet ultrasound, also called an AFAST test, creates images of the inside of your pet's body using sound waves. Most of our vets can do an AFAST test at your location. A pet ultrasound test can help your vet find out what's wrong with your pet that a regular checkup might miss.

Request appointment

What is a Mobile Pet Ultrasound?

Mobile pet ultrasound refers to vet ultrasound services provided conveniently at a client's home or farm setting by using small, battery-operated ultrasound machines that produce high-resolution diagnostic images of internal anatomical structures through the completely noninvasive use of targeted high-frequency sound waves.

This allows detailed organ evaluation and anomaly detection to be performed in a comforting, familiar environment for the animal instead of making them go to a clinic, which is especially helpful for anxious, elderly, or mobility-limited pets.

What to Expect During Mobile Pet Ultrasound

For your appointment, our experienced ultrasound tech will arrive with our specialty ultrasound equipment at your home or location of choice. Your pet simply lies or sits comfortably while ultrasound gel is applied to their fur. The wand-like ultrasound transducer is gently maneuvered over their body to obtain diagnostic images of the abdomen, thorax, heart, bladder, and more. Real-time black-and-white images appear onscreen. You're welcome to watch the ultrasound being performed and see your pet's internal anatomy! The scan is quick, and most pets tolerate it well without sedation.

How Mobile Ultrasound Benefits Your Pet

Stress-free in-home ultrasound avoids travel to clinics, which can be difficult for anxious or ill pets. Images help detect many disorders earlier, when treatment is most effective. Ultrasound guides precision procedures like cyst drainages or biopsies in familiar surroundings.

It provides insight into the stage and progression of known conditions during cancer care or chronic disease monitoring. Overall, mobile ultrasound makes it easier, more comfortable, and more consistent for your pet. Bring advanced imaging convenience to your pet.
