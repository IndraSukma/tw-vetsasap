Our doctors can perform the following services conveniently in your home

Puppy and Kitten Wellness

Your puppy or kitten deserves the best start in life, and that is why we offer comprehensive wellness visits to support their health and happiness. Our wellness visits include physical exams, vaccinations, parasite prevention, and much more.

Sick Visit

Your pet deserves the best care possible, especially when they are sick. Thatâ€™s why our mobile vets bring the clinic to your home, saving you time and hassle. Contact us now and let us help your dog or cat feel better.

Wellness Exams

Keep your pet healthy and happy, with in-home wellness exams. Your vet will check its vital signs, lifestyle, nutrition, and behavior. Book an appointment today and get a free pet care kit.

In-Home Pet Euthanasia

If your pet is nearing the end of their life, you deserve to have a caring and honest conversation with one of our compassionate veterinarians. They will help you assess your pet's quality of life and guide you through the best options for a peaceful farewell. Contact us today and let us support you and your pet in this difficult time.

Virtual Vet Visit

Donâ€™t let your pet suffer in silence, get instant online access to a licensed veterinarian that can answer your questions about your petâ€™s health, behavior, or nutrition. Whether you need a follow-up consultation or a second opinion, our telemedicine service is here for you. Book now and give the best care to your furry friend.

Quality-of-Life Consultation

Your pet deserves the best care, especially when they are getting old or sick. That's why we offer Quality of Life Care services, which include gentle consultations and end-of-life services for pets at your place. Contact us now and we'll help you through this tough time.

Pet Nutritional Planning

Your pet can enjoy a longer and healthier life by staying at a weight that is ideal for them. By feeding your pet well and keeping their weight in check, you can reduce their chances of developing high blood pressure, diabetes, respiratory issues, and more

Vaccination

Dog and cat vaccines are part of providing essential, preventative care for your petâ€™s long-term health and well-being. We offer all essential and optional vaccines for your pet at your place.

Pet Behavior Counseling

Our veterinary doctors can offer personalized behavioral advice based on your petâ€™s age, breed, and health and can help you with any general behavior queries or issues you have about your pet.

International Travel Certificate

International travel with your pet usually requires a check-up by a USDA-certified vet and different rules based on the country you're going to, plus getting a health certificate approved by a vet. Our traveling vets are USDA-certified, have the power to issue a USDA-certified pet travel certificate, and will come to you!

Virtual Domestic Travel Certificate

When traveling with your pet within the US, there are different pet health rules depending on where you're going. Some states require a check-up by a USDA-accredited vet, current shots, lab testing, and getting an approved domestic pet health travel certificate, also called a Certificate of Veterinary Inspection.

Blood Work

Vets ASAP is ready to do in-home pet blood work to monitor your cat or dog's health, or for diagnosis purposes. Our Fear-FreeÂ® vets will take your pet's blood in your comfy home.

Neurological Examination

During a comprehensive in-home Wellness Exam or Sick Visit, our traveling vets can check your petâ€™s signs and brain function along with a complete nose-to-tail check-up of your petâ€™s wellness.

Orthopedic Examination

As part of your pet's Wellness Exam, your Vets ASAP veterinarian will check your pet's orthopedic and neurological health. We can check how your pet's bones and joints work in different ways. Pets often hide their problems very well, so it is important for you to tell us about any changes you've noticed at your place.

Pet Pain Management

Whether your pet is having short-term pain or long-term aches and pains as they age or dealing with a chronic condition, our traveling vets will visit you and help your pet feel better.

Dermatologic Examination

During a Wellness or Sick Exam, our traveling vets can talk about how food or environmental allergies affect your pet and what to do for your pet. Pet allergy testing, diet tests, and referrals to a vet allergy expert might be helpful.

Electrocardiogram (ECG)

An important diagnostic tool to assess your petâ€™s health, an ECG measures the electrical activity of the heart and can spot any irregular or abnormal heartbeats. Depending on location, our vets can perform dog and cat ECGs in your home or the Vets ASAP van.

Microchipping

At-home cat and dog microchipping is an easy, stress-free, and long-term way to track down your lost pet. A pet microchip can work for up to 25 years and stay in place permanently, unlike other ID methods like a tag or a collar.

Veterinary Diagnostic Imaging

Vet X-ray is used to find out what's wrong with your pet. X-rays may be recommended when your vet needs a clear picture of what's inside your pet. Our care teams can do X-rays at your home or in our Vets ASAP van, wherever you are.

Mobile Pet Ultrasound

A pet ultrasound, also called an AFAST test, creates images of the inside of your pet's body using sound waves. Most of our vets can do an AFAST test at your location. A pet ultrasound test can help your vet find out what's wrong with your pet that a regular checkup might miss.
